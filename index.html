1. Introduction Shodai ↑ Back to Top

Created: 10/10/2024

by: Ataullah Akram

Introducing Shodai, a comprehensive and visually captivating e-commerce mobile app UI template, designed to simplify online shopping while elevating the user experience. With 159+ professionally designed screens, Shodai is the ultimate solution for creating a powerful and intuitive shopping platform tailored to any e-commerce business.

Shodai covers the entire customer journey—from browsing and product discovery to secure checkout and order tracking. It also features a Day View and Night View, ensuring a seamless and visually pleasing experience at any time.

How to install Figma Video

Step 1: Go to Figma on Academic Software and click on the button ‘Download Figma’ to download the installer.

Step 2: Open the .exe installer file from your downloads folder to start the installation.

Step 3: Once the installation is completed, the startup screen of Figma will appear.

How to change the image

Step 1: Select the layer.

Step 2: Click on “Upload from computer” and replace your particular image

How to edit text style

Step 1: Select the layer.
